RULES---------

this is completely a game of chance, the only choices you have increase or decrease your risk of losing money. but higher risks lead to higher rewards

- you need to add a bet to play

- once a bet is added you can choose to add a pair plus bonus

- pair plus bonus means you will win extra money if u have a pair or better in your hand. However, if you add the pair plus bonus and do not get a pair or better, you will win less money than normally

- once you have decided on your bet, press ante-up this will lock in your bet and show you your hand

- here you can decide if you want to play or fold.

- if you play, you will need to match your bet (effectively doubling your bet)

- e.g if you bet 200 and then press play, you are actually betting 400 and will lose that much if you lose

- if you win however, the 400 will get multiplied and given back to you how much it is multiplied by depends on if you applied the pair plus bonus

- on the other hand, if you fold, you will simply lose the money you put in. - there is no way to gain money if you fold but you will lose less money than if you played and lost.

e.g if you put 200 in and folded you would lose 200. but if you put 200 in played and lost, you would lose 400.
