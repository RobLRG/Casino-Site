RULES---------

this goal is to reach a score of 21, if you go over 21 you are bust and lose, if neither player reaches 21, the closest to it without going over wins. if both players are bust then it is a draw.

- you need to add a bet to play

- your bet is displayed in the bottom right

- once a bet is added you can choose to "hit" or "hold"

- hitting will give you another card, increasing your score.

- your score is displayed underneath the play buttons

- holding will end the round, so only do this once you are happy with your hand

- once the round is over, the value of both players hands will be compared and a winner will be found. if you win, your bet gets doubled and returned to you. if you lose, you lose your bet

- the value of kings,queens and jacks are all the same: 10

- the value of aces are 11, however if your score is above 21, the value of an ace will be 1 instead

- you cant see the other players cards but you can see how many cards they have, use this to decide to hit or hold.
